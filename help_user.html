Quiz App Tutorial



1. Welcome! This is the starting page of the quiz app!
You will see your current location and several question marker in the screen!

2. Click on the question and check on the answer you think is correct!
Then click submit. Result will automatically popup telling you the result!

3. The popup will further tells you that the answer has been successfully upload.

4. In the Resource --> App statistic tab, you can view your current score and comparing to other user.
There are two App statistic you can choose, the first one will load the statistic page in a new tab, the second will load the page here!
The "get location" is where you can relocate your current position. You can also check your user ID and current time in the below content.

This is the end of the tutorial! Hope you enjoy the quiz app!
